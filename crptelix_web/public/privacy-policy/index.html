Privacy Policy

We built Cryptelix around a simple idea: you should be able to track your portfolio without handing over more data than is strictly necessary. This document explains what we collect, why, and how it stays safe.

Data we collect

No KYC. No full name. No phone number or address. Cryptelix asks only for what it needs to function — nothing more.

What Why we need it How long we keep it
Email address Authentication, account access, and critical system notifications only. Until you close your account.
Binance API keys (read-only) Pulling historical balances, trades, and metrics to build your dashboard. Encrypted at rest; deleted the moment you remove them.

API key rules

When you connect your Binance account, we only work with read-only API keys. There are two hard rules we enforce technically, not just by policy:

  • Read permission only. We accept keys with the Enable Reading scope. That's it.
  • Trading and withdrawals are blocked. Any key with Spot Trading, Futures, or Withdrawal permissions is automatically rejected the moment it is submitted.
Important

Never enable trading or withdrawal permissions when generating your key on Binance. We will never ask you to do so, under any circumstances.

What we don't do

  • We don't store IP addresses for tracking or geolocation.
  • We don't build ad profiles or share data with advertising networks.
  • We don't use session-replay scripts, fingerprinting, or covert trackers of any kind.
  • We don't share anything with institutional brokers or data aggregators.
